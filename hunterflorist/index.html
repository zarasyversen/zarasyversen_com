Welcome to Hunter Florist

At Hunter florist we like to offer and always have a good selection of house plants, garden, patio plant's, cacti and succulent's. We also always stock a variety of types of Orchid, that are a very easy option for you to have in your home. They require very little attention, and will give you showy flower blooms for months at time as well as making an ideal gift.

Flowers for any occasion

We of course also stock an abundance of good grade, quality, fresh cut flower's from all over the world, and when in season we like to promote as much U.K. grown flower and foliage as possible. We can make you a simple cheer yourself up bouquet or a beautiful arrangement for a special occasion totally suitable to your needs.

We pride ourselves on those times in your life, where your flower arrangements are such a big part of your day. If it's to seal your love to each other, or to say farewell to a loved one.
